    Priority Queue

  • characterized by the remove the maximum and insert operations

  • Bottom-up reheapify (swim): If the heap order is violated because a node's key becomes larger than that node's parents key, fix by exchanging the node with its parent.
  • Top-down heapify (sink): a node's key becomes smaller than one or both of that node's children's keys, fix by exchanging the node with the larger of its two children
  • Insert: add key to bottom (end of array) and swim up
  • Remove max: exchange bottom children (end of array) with root, remove new bottom children (max), sink new root down

      • Heapsort

  • insert all the keys to be sorted into a minimum-oriented priority queue, then repeatedly use remove the minimum to remove them all in order
